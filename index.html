Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about Leverra Sri Damansara

Common questions buyers ask before booking a private viewing. Need more detail? Reach our agents directly.

Is Leverra Sri Damansara freehold?
Yes — Leverra Sri Damansara is offered as a freehold residential development, an attractive feature for both own-stay buyers and long-term property investors in Kuala Lumpur.
How far is Leverra Sri Damansara from the MRT?
Leverra is approximately 500m from Sri Damansara West MRT station, with a covered walkway connection that makes the daily commute convenient regardless of weather.
What unit layouts are available at Leverra Sri Damansara?
Three primary layouts are offered:
  • Type A — 2 bedrooms / 2 bathrooms / ~728 sqft
  • Type B — 3 bedrooms / 2 bathrooms / ~903 sqft
  • Type C — 4 bedrooms / 3 bathrooms / ~1145 sqft
What facilities are included at Leverra Sri Damansara?
Facilities include a 50m lap pool, fully equipped gymnasium, landscaped gardens, 24/7 security, retail and F&B convenience, and pedestrian-friendly connectivity to the surrounding township.
How do I book a viewing or pre-sale briefing?
You can reach our professional agents Kenneth Lai at +60 16-628 7357 or Agnes Chee at +60 12-289 5038. WhatsApp is the fastest way to confirm an appointment and receive the latest availability and pricing information.
Is Sri Damansara a good area for property investment in Malaysia?
Bandar Sri Damansara is an established township with mature amenities, MRT connectivity, and strong access to LDP, NKVE and DUKE highways. Its proximity to Kuala Lumpur's commercial hubs and Petaling Jaya makes it a strategic location for both rental yield and long-term capital appreciation.
